# Benchmark Targets — Partner Performance

These benchmarks represent Rev A Manufacturing's expectations for manufacturing partners. Use these targets when evaluating partner performance and setting improvement goals.

## Quality Benchmarks

| Metric | Minimum Acceptable | Target | Best-in-Class |
|--------|-------------------|--------|---------------|
| Defect rate | < 2.0% | < 0.5% | < 0.1% |
| NCR rate (per order) | < 0.3 | < 0.1 | 0 |
| First article pass rate | > 80% | > 95% | 100% |
| Customer returns (per year) | < 3 | 0-1 | 0 |
| Rework frequency | Occasionally | Rarely | Never |

## Delivery Benchmarks

| Metric | Minimum Acceptable | Target | Best-in-Class |
|--------|-------------------|--------|---------------|
| On-time delivery rate | > 85% | > 95% | 100% |
| Average days late (when late) | < 7 days | < 3 days | 0 days |
| Lead time accuracy | Within 5 days of quote | Within 2 days | Quoted or earlier |
| Milestone updates | Sometimes | Usually | Always |

## Cost Benchmarks

| Metric | Minimum Acceptable | Target | Best-in-Class |
|--------|-------------------|--------|---------------|
| Price vs. market | Within 15% | At market or below | Below market |
| Price stability | < 2 increases/year | < 1 increase/year | No increases |
| Hidden costs | < 10% of order | < 5% of order | None |
| Tooling cost accuracy | Within 15% of quote | Within 5% | At or under quote |

## Communication Benchmarks

| Metric | Minimum Acceptable | Target | Best-in-Class |
|--------|-------------------|--------|---------------|
| Response time | < 2 business days | < 8 hours | < 4 hours |
| Proactive updates | Sometimes | Usually | Always |
| Language barriers | Minor | None | None |
| Issue resolution | Average (2-3 days) | Fast (same/next day) | Fast (same day) |
| Dedicated contact | Preferred | Yes | Yes, with backup |

## Overall Grade Targets

| Partner Type | Minimum Grade | Target Grade |
|-------------|--------------|--------------|
| Primary / preferred partner | B | A |
| Secondary partner | C | B |
| New partner (first year) | C | B |
| Trial partner | D | C |

## Improvement Expectations

When a partner scores below target:

| Current Grade | Expected Improvement Timeline | Review Frequency |
|--------------|------------------------------|-----------------|
| A | Maintain | Annually |
| B | Improve to A within 12 months | Semi-annually |
| C | Improve to B within 6 months | Quarterly |
| D | Improve to C within 3 months | Monthly |
| F | Immediate corrective action or replace | Weekly until resolved |

## Partner Retention Criteria

Partners must maintain a minimum grade of C to remain active. Two consecutive quarterly grades of D or below triggers a partner replacement review with Senior PM and Donovan Weber.
