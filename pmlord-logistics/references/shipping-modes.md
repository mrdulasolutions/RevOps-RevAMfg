# Shipping Modes Reference

## International Shipping (China to USA)

### Air Express
- **Carriers:** DHL Express, FedEx International Priority, UPS Worldwide Express
- **Transit Time:** 3-5 business days
- **Cost Range:** $6-12 per kg
- **Max Weight:** Varies by carrier (typically up to 70 kg per piece)
- **Best For:** Urgent orders, small/light packages, samples, replacement parts
- **Customs:** Carrier handles customs brokerage
- **Tracking:** Real-time door-to-door tracking
- **Insurance:** Included up to declared value limits

### Air Freight
- **Carriers:** Freight forwarders booking on major airlines (China Southern, Air China, Cathay Pacific, etc.)
- **Transit Time:** 5-7 business days (airport to airport) + 2-3 days for customs and last-mile
- **Cost Range:** $3-6 per kg
- **Max Weight:** No practical limit
- **Best For:** Time-sensitive shipments, moderate volume (100-2000 kg)
- **Customs:** Customs broker required
- **Tracking:** Flight-level tracking, less granular than express
- **Insurance:** Separate cargo insurance recommended

### Sea Freight - Full Container Load (FCL)
- **Carriers:** COSCO, Maersk, MSC, Evergreen, ONE, Yang Ming
- **Transit Time:** 25-35 days (port to port) + 3-5 days for drayage and customs
- **Cost Range:** $3,000-8,000 per 20ft container; $5,000-12,000 per 40ft container
- **Container Sizes:** 20ft (33 CBM), 40ft (67 CBM), 40ft HC (76 CBM)
- **Best For:** Large volume, cost-sensitive, non-urgent orders
- **Customs:** Customs broker required, ISF filing mandatory (24 hrs before loading)
- **Tracking:** Vessel tracking, port-level updates
- **Insurance:** Marine cargo insurance required

### Sea Freight - Less than Container Load (LCL)
- **Carriers:** Same ocean carriers, consolidated by freight forwarder
- **Transit Time:** 30-40 days (includes consolidation/deconsolidation time)
- **Cost Range:** $50-120 per CBM (cubic meter)
- **Min Charge:** Typically 1 CBM minimum
- **Best For:** Smaller shipments not justifying full container
- **Customs:** Same as FCL
- **Tracking:** Less frequent updates than FCL
- **Insurance:** Marine cargo insurance required

### Split Shipment (Air + Sea)
- **Method:** Ship urgent portion by air, remainder by sea
- **Transit Time:** Air portion 5-7 days, sea portion 25-40 days
- **Cost:** Blended rate between air and sea
- **Best For:** Orders where partial delivery satisfies immediate need

## Domestic Shipping (Rev A to Customer)

### Ground
- **Carriers:** FedEx Ground, UPS Ground, USPS Priority Mail
- **Transit Time:** 2-5 business days (depends on distance)
- **Cost Range:** $0.50-2.00 per lb
- **Best For:** Standard domestic deliveries, cost-sensitive

### Air (Domestic)
- **Carriers:** FedEx Express, UPS Next Day/2-Day, USPS Priority Mail Express
- **Transit Time:** 1-2 business days
- **Cost Range:** $2-8 per lb
- **Best For:** Urgent domestic deliveries

### LTL Freight (Domestic)
- **Carriers:** XPO Logistics, Old Dominion, Estes Express, FedEx Freight
- **Transit Time:** 2-5 business days
- **Cost Range:** Quoted per shipment based on weight, dimensions, freight class
- **Best For:** Palletized shipments, heavy/bulky orders (150+ lbs)

### FTL Freight (Domestic)
- **Carriers:** Various, booked through freight brokers
- **Transit Time:** 1-3 days (direct routes)
- **Cost Range:** $1,500-5,000 depending on distance
- **Best For:** Large volume requiring full truck
