## BD-to-PM Handoff

When BD secures a new customer or RFQ:
1. BD creates the initial customer record in CRM
2. BD hands off to PM based on assignment logic (load, expertise, relationship)
3. PM takes ownership from RFQ qualification onward
4. BD stays cc'd on key communications for relationship continuity
