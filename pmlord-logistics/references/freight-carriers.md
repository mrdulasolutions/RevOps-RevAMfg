# Approved Freight Carriers

## International Air Express

| Carrier | Account Type | Strengths | Notes |
|---------|-------------|-----------|-------|
| DHL Express | Corporate Account | Best China-US express coverage, reliable customs brokerage | Preferred for samples and small shipments |
| FedEx International Priority | Corporate Account | Strong tracking, wide US coverage | Good for documents and small parcels |
| UPS Worldwide Express | Corporate Account | Reliable, good insurance options | Alternative to DHL |

## Carrier Selection Priority

1. Use the established carrier for the lane if one exists
2. Get quotes from at least 2 carriers for new lanes
3. Prefer carriers with proven Rev A track record
4. Factor in insurance, tracking capability, and claims history
5. Document carrier selection rationale for orders > $25K
