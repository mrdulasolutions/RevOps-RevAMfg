# PM Lifecycle Stages — Rev A Manufacturing

## Complete Lifecycle (RFQ to Delivery)

### Stage 1: RFQ Intake
- Receive RFQ via email, website, or CRM
- Parse and extract structured data
- Assign to PM
- Log in CRM

### Stage 2: RFQ Qualification (Gate Check)
- New vs returning customer check
- Capability match against Rev A services
- Complexity scoring (1-5)
- Capacity check against current load
- Credit check readiness
- Decision: PROCEED / CONDITIONAL / DECLINE

### Stage 3: Customer Onboarding (if new)
- Collect company info
- Verify legitimacy
- Set up in CRM
- Assign PM
- Establish credit terms

### Stage 4: Quote Generation
- Cost estimation (material + labor + tooling + finishing + assembly + overhead)
- Margin calculation
- Lead time estimation
- Tooling cost separation
- Shipping estimate
- Quote package assembly

### Stage 5: Quote Submission & Follow-up
- Send quote to customer
- Track response
- Follow up if no response within 3 business days
- Handle negotiation / revision

### Stage 6: Order Acceptance
- PO received and acknowledged
- Order confirmation sent to customer
- Order entered in tracking system

### Stage 7: Manufacturing Package
- Standardize specs for China partner
- Package drawings and requirements
- Translation notes for key terms
- IP protection measures
- Send to manufacturing partner

### Stage 8: Manufacturing Tracking
- 10-milestone tracking
- Delay detection and management
- Quality checkpoint scheduling
- First article approval

### Stage 9: Shipping (China to Rev A)
- International freight coordination
- Customs documentation
- Tracking and ETA management

### Stage 10: Incoming Inspection
- Receive goods at Rev A
- Inspect against specs
- Dimensional checks, material verification, finish inspection
- Disposition: ACCEPT / REJECT / HOLD
- NCR if non-conforming

### Stage 11: Quality Gate
- Final quality verification
- Pre-ship inspection
- Customer-specific requirements check

### Stage 12: Repackaging (if inspect-and-forward)
- Repackage per customer requirements
- Labeling
- Documentation inclusion

### Stage 13: Outbound Shipping
- Domestic freight coordination
- Tracking info to customer
- Delivery confirmation

### Stage 14: Order Close
- Delivery confirmed
- Customer satisfaction follow-up (T+3 days)
- Order closed in system
- Audit trail complete
