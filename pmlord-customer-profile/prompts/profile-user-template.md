# Profile User Template

Variables for customer profile building and maintenance.

## Company Information

- **{{CUSTOMER_COMPANY}}** — Full company name
- **{{CUSTOMER_ID}}** — Internal customer ID
- **{{CUSTOMER_WEBSITE}}** — Company website
- **{{CUSTOMER_INDUSTRY}}** — Industry/sector
- **{{CUSTOMER_SIZE}}** — Company size (employees)
- **{{CUSTOMER_LOCATION}}** — Primary location
- **{{CUSTOMER_DESCRIPTION}}** — What the customer does / makes

## Contacts

- **{{PRIMARY_CONTACT_NAME}}** — Primary contact name
- **{{PRIMARY_CONTACT_TITLE}}** — Primary contact title
- **{{PRIMARY_CONTACT_EMAIL}}** — Primary contact email
- **{{PRIMARY_CONTACT_PHONE}}** — Primary contact phone
- **{{SECONDARY_CONTACTS}}** — List of additional contacts (name, title, email, role)
- **{{DECISION_MAKER}}** — Person with PO/purchase authority
- **{{TECHNICAL_CONTACT}}** — Person for engineering/technical questions
- **{{QUALITY_CONTACT}}** — Person for quality/inspection matters

## Order History Summary

- **{{TOTAL_ORDERS}}** — Total number of completed orders
- **{{LIFETIME_VALUE}}** — Total revenue from this customer
- **{{AVERAGE_ORDER_VALUE}}** — Average order value
- **{{FIRST_ORDER_DATE}}** — Date of first order
- **{{LAST_ORDER_DATE}}** — Date of most recent order
- **{{ORDER_FREQUENCY}}** — Typical order pattern (weekly, monthly, quarterly, project-based)
- **{{TYPICAL_PARTS}}** — Most commonly ordered part types
- **{{TYPICAL_PROCESSES}}** — Most commonly used manufacturing processes
- **{{TYPICAL_MATERIALS}}** — Most commonly requested materials
- **{{TYPICAL_QUANTITIES}}** — Typical order quantities

## Quality Profile

- **{{QUALITY_TIER}}** — Quality expectation level (Standard, High, Critical)
- **{{REQUIRED_DOCS}}** — Quality documentation always required (CoC, FAIR, PPAP, etc.)
- **{{TOLERANCE_PROFILE}}** — Typical tolerance expectations
- **{{TOTAL_NCRS}}** — Total NCRs filed
- **{{NCR_DETAILS}}** — Summary of NCR history
- **{{FIRST_PASS_RATE}}** — First-time acceptance rate
- **{{QUALITY_NOTES}}** — Special quality requirements or preferences

## Payment Profile

- **{{PAYMENT_TERMS}}** — Current payment terms
- **{{CREDIT_TIER}}** — Credit tier (1-5)
- **{{ON_TIME_RATE}}** — Payment on-time rate
- **{{OUTSTANDING_BALANCE}}** — Current outstanding balance
- **{{PAYMENT_HISTORY_SUMMARY}}** — Payment behavior summary
- **{{PAYMENT_NOTES}}** — Special payment arrangements or issues

## Relationship Intelligence

- **{{ASSIGNED_PM}}** — Current assigned PM
- **{{BD_REP}}** — BD rep (if applicable)
- **{{RELATIONSHIP_STRENGTH}}** — Strong / Moderate / Developing / At Risk
- **{{COMMUNICATION_PREF}}** — Preferred communication method
- **{{PRICING_SENSITIVITY}}** — Price-driven / Value-driven / Relationship-driven
- **{{COMPETITORS}}** — Known competitors for this account
- **{{GROWTH_POTENTIAL}}** — Growth assessment (High / Moderate / Low / Declining)
- **{{UPCOMING_OPPORTUNITIES}}** — Known future projects or orders
- **{{RELATIONSHIP_NOTES}}** — PM notes on the relationship

## Customer Tier

- **{{CUSTOMER_TIER}}** — Platinum / Gold / Silver / Bronze / New
- **{{TIER_RATIONALE}}** — Reason for tier assignment
- **{{LAST_REVIEW_DATE}}** — Date of last profile review
- **{{NEXT_REVIEW_DATE}}** — Scheduled next review
