## China — Special Considerations for Rev A

China is Rev A's primary manufacturing partner country. Most commercial manufacturing is permitted, but:

1. **Always screen the partner** against Entity List (hundreds of Chinese entities listed)
2. **Military end-use rule** applies — if Rev A knows or has reason to know the final product will be used by Chinese military, a license is required even for EAR99 items
3. **Country Group D:1** — many controlled items (anything other than EAR99) require a license for China
4. **Deemed export** — Chinese nationals working at Rev A's facility who access controlled technical data = deemed export to China
5. **De minimis** — Items manufactured in China with >25% US-origin controlled content are subject to EAR if re-exported
