# Rev A Manufacturing — Capabilities Matrix

## Production Machining
- CNC milling (3-axis, 4-axis, 5-axis)
- CNC turning / lathe
- Swiss-type machining
- Multi-axis simultaneous machining
- **Materials:** aluminum, steel, stainless steel, brass, copper, titanium, plastics (Delrin, PEEK, nylon)
- **Tolerances:** Standard +/- 0.005", precision +/- 0.001", ultra-precision +/- 0.0005"

## Injection Tooling & Molding
- Mold design and engineering
- Tooling fabrication (P20, H13, S136, NAK80)
- Injection molding (thermoplastics)
- Insert molding
- Overmolding
- **Materials:** ABS, PC, PP, PE, nylon, POM, PEEK, TPE/TPU
- **Mold life:** prototype (1K-10K shots), production (100K-1M+ shots)

## Prototyping
- Rapid prototyping
- 3D printing (FDM, SLA, SLS)
- Short-run production (1-500 units)
- Bridge tooling
- Functional prototypes

## Sheet Metal
- Laser cutting
- CNC punching
- Bending / forming
- Welding (TIG, MIG, spot)
- Stamping
- **Materials:** steel, stainless steel, aluminum, copper, brass
- **Thickness:** 0.5mm - 12mm typical

## Finishing
- Anodizing (Type II, Type III / hard coat)
- Plating (chrome, nickel, zinc, gold)
- Powder coating
- Painting (wet spray, electrostatic)
- Polishing (mirror, brushed, bead blast)
- Passivation
- Black oxide
- Heat treatment

## Assembly
- Mechanical assembly
- Sub-assembly
- Kitting
- Packaging
- Functional testing
- Labeling / marking (laser, silk screen, pad print)

## Capability Limits (DECLINE if)
- Casting (die cast, sand cast, investment cast) — refer to partner
- Forging — refer to partner
- PCB / electronics manufacturing — refer to partner
- Textile / soft goods — outside scope
- Chemicals / hazardous materials — outside scope
