# Escalation Matrix — Rev A Manufacturing

## Escalation Levels

### Level 1: PM Self-Resolution
- Minor delays (<1 week)
- Cosmetic quality issues (within spec tolerance)
- Customer questions about order status
- Routine manufacturing partner communication

### Level 2: Senior PM (Ray Yeh / Harley Scott)
- Quality issues potentially affecting delivery
- Delivery delays exceeding 1 week
- Customer dissatisfaction (non-critical)
- Partner performance issues (first occurrence)
- Capacity conflicts between orders
- Complex quoting decisions

### Level 3: Donovan Weber (President)
- Quality issues affecting customer relationship
- Delivery delays exceeding 2 weeks beyond committed date
- Customer complaints about quality or service
- Partner performance below C grade (sustained)
- RFQ for capability outside Rev A scope (strategic decision)
- Credit/payment issues
- Legal or contractual issues
- New partner evaluation / qualification
- Pricing exceptions beyond standard margin guidelines

| Trigger | Level | Action |
|---------|-------|--------|

| Delivery delay >7 days | 2 | Notify Senior PM |

| Quote >$50K | 2 | Senior PM review before sending |
