## Ambiguous Terms — Use With Care

| English Term | Potential Confusion | Recommended Approach |
|-------------|-------------------|---------------------|
| "Finish" | Can mean surface treatment OR final state | Specify: "surface finish" (表面处理) vs. "completed" (完成) |
| "Stock" | Raw material vs. inventory | Specify: "raw material" (原材料) vs. "inventory" (库存) |
| "Die" | Stamping die vs. die casting mold | Specify the process explicitly |
| "Tool" | Cutting tool vs. mold/die | Use "cutting tool" (刀具) or "mold" (模具) |
| "Lot" | Production batch vs. quantity | Use "batch" (批次) with quantity specified |
| "Run" | Production run vs. machine operation | Use "production batch" (生产批次) |
