# Shipping Cost Estimates

## International Shipping (China to US)

### Ocean Freight

| Shipment Size | Cost Range | Transit Time | Notes |
|--------------|-----------|-------------|-------|
| Small (< 100 lbs / < 0.5 CBM) | $200-500 | 20-30 days | LCL (less than container load) |
| Medium (100-500 lbs / 0.5-2 CBM) | $400-1,200 | 20-30 days | LCL |
| Large (500-2,000 lbs / 2-5 CBM) | $800-2,500 | 20-30 days | LCL or partial container |
| Full container (20') | $2,500-5,000 | 18-25 days | FCL |
| Full container (40') | $4,000-8,000 | 18-25 days | FCL |

### Air Freight

| Shipment Size | Cost Range | Transit Time | Notes |
|--------------|-----------|-------------|-------|
| Small (< 50 lbs) | $150-400 | 3-5 days | Express courier (DHL/FedEx/UPS) |
| Medium (50-250 lbs) | $400-1,500 | 4-7 days | Air cargo |
| Large (250-1,000 lbs) | $1,500-5,000 | 4-7 days | Air cargo |
| Very large (1,000+ lbs) | $4-8 per lb | 5-7 days | Air cargo, quote individually |

### Additional International Costs

| Item | Cost |
|------|------|
| Customs brokerage | $150-350 per shipment |
| Customs duties (typical for machined parts) | 2-5% of declared value |
| Customs duties (typical for molded parts) | 3-6% of declared value |
| Insurance (optional) | 0.5-1.5% of declared value |
| Fumigation (wood packaging) | $100-300 |
| Inspection at origin (third party) | $200-500 |

## Shipping Cost Estimation Rules

1. **Estimate weight** from part weight x quantity + packaging (add 10-20% for packaging materials)
2. **Default to ocean freight** unless timeline requires air
3. **Add Rev A handling fee** of 5-10% on shipping costs
4. **Include customs duties** in the shipping estimate unless the customer has their own import broker
5. **Insurance** is recommended for shipments over $10,000 declared value
6. **Packaging** costs are included in the repackaging line item, not shipping

## Incoterms

Rev A typically quotes:
- **DDP (Delivered Duty Paid)** — Rev A handles all shipping and customs; customer receives goods at their door. Most common for domestic customers.
- **FOB Rev A** — Customer arranges pickup from Rev A facility after inspection/repackaging. Less common but used for large customers with their own freight.

Note: International shipping terms between Rev A and China partners are separate and not customer-facing.
