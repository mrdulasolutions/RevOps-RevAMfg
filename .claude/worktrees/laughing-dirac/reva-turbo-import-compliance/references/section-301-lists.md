# Section 301 Tariffs — China

Additional tariffs imposed on Chinese-origin goods under Section 301 of the Trade Act of 1974.

## Current Tariff Lists

| List | Additional Tariff | Approx. HTS Lines | Effective Date | Coverage |
|------|-------------------|--------------------|----------------|----------|
| **List 1** | 25% | ~818 | Jul 6, 2018 | Industrial machinery, electronics, aerospace |
| **List 2** | 25% | ~279 | Aug 23, 2018 | Semiconductors, chemicals, plastics, motors |
| **List 3** | 25% | ~5,745 | Sep 24, 2018 (10%), May 10, 2019 (25%) | Broad — industrial, furniture, textiles, food |
| **List 4A** | 7.5% | ~3,805 | Feb 14, 2020 | Consumer goods, some industrial |

**List 4B** was proposed but suspended.

### Possibly List 4A (7.5% additional):

- Certain consumer-facing products

## Exclusions

Some products have been granted temporary exclusions from Section 301 tariffs. Exclusions:
- Are product-specific (by HTS 10-digit code and product description)
- Have expiration dates (must be renewed)
- Must be claimed at time of entry
- Can be applied retroactively (refund of duties paid)

**CHECK:** Before paying Section 301 duties, verify whether an exclusion exists for the specific HTS code at ustr.gov.

## How to Determine Which List

1. Look up the 8-digit HTS code
2. Check against USTR's published annexes for each list
3. TradeInsights.ai API returns this automatically
4. Customs broker systems (ACE) flag this at entry

## Calculating Total Duty

```
Item value (FOB):                    $10,000
MFN duty rate (e.g., 5%):           $500
Section 301 tariff (e.g., 25%):     $2,500
MPF (0.3464%):                      $34.64
────────────────────────────────────
Total duties on $10,000 item:        $3,034.64
Effective rate:                      30.35%
```

**NOTE:** Section 301 rates and coverage are subject to change by executive action. Always verify current rates.
