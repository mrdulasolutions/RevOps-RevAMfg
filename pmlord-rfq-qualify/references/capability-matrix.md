## Capabilities NOT Offered

The following are outside Rev A Manufacturing's current capability set:

- Die casting (aluminum, zinc)
- Forging
- Investment casting
- Extrusion
- PCB fabrication and SMT assembly
- Ceramic manufacturing
- Glass manufacturing
- Composite layup (carbon fiber, fiberglass)
- Large-format welded fabrication (structural steel)
- Textile/soft goods manufacturing

If an RFQ requires any of these, the capability gate should return NO MATCH.
